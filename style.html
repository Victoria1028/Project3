Influences

Brody is strongly interested in type. Brody strongly encourages his students, coworkers, and fellow designers to experiment and to “embrace risk-taking.”

He enjoys pushing what design can be and rearranging elements while also adhering to an underlying design system (such as a grid). Plays a lot with sans-serif typefaces.

Early in his career he was especially influenced by Punk, because punk rock was at its prime when he was growing up in London. He would earn his first bit of recognition thanks to his designs for punk record covers.

He drew inspiration from Dadaism- as he liked the techniques that they used- and pop art (thank you bright colors).
