Neville Brody is a London-based graphic designer and typographer. He is world-renowned and has worked with several large companies throughout his life, including Dior, Supreme, Samsung, Mayo Clinic, and many many more. To this day, despite his many obligations and work avenues, he is a very hands-on designer for his company Brody Associates, based in London.
